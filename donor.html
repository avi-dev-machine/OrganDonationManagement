Frequently Asked Questions

Will donation affect my funeral arrangements?

No, organ and tissue donation doesn't interfere with open-casket funeral arrangements.

Is there a cost to donate?

No, there is no cost to the donor or their family for organ and tissue donation.

Can I choose who receives my organs?

While directed donation is possible in some cases, organs are typically allocated based on medical compatibility and urgency.
